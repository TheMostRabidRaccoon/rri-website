The Courthouse

Open Evidence — High Justice

“Cases come to us.”
Position
Northeast, elevated — a few steps up to reach the doors. Authority is spatial.
Materials
Board-formed concrete, aged copper detailing, heavy oak interior.

The Amendments are carved into the exterior stone facade, readable from the path. Open Evidence’s bench: raised, dark wood, single lamp, citations always present. A brass scale. Verdict ledger open on the stand. One empty chair at the defense table, always.

// The chair has never been empty. It just looks that way.
